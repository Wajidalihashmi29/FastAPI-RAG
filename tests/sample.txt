Title: The Future of Artificial Intelligence

Key Developments in AI:
- Machine Learning: Algorithms that allow systems to learn from data without explicit programming.
- Natural Language Processing: Enabling machines to understand and interact using human language.
- Robotics: AI-driven robots are being used in manufacturing, surgery, and even customer service.
